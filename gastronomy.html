Gastronomy

Paella valenciana

Typical of Valencia, it is the most famous Spanish dish. Whether you like meat, seafood or both, there is a paella for every taste.

Tapas

If you can't decide what to eat, why not have it all? Tapas range from jamón ibérico to gambas al ajillo, they are certain to leave you satisfied!

Tortilla de patata

Probably the most famous omelette in the world, it is a simple and nutritious dish. So nutritious, it said a general invented it to feed soldiers!

Cocido madrileño

This substantial dish is very popular in winter. It goes back to the Spanish Jews from the Middle Ages and was later incorporated in the national tradition.

Pulpo a la gallega

Originating in Galicia, this dish is traditionally cooked in the open and the octopus is boiled three times before adding the spices that make it so delicious!
