Main Attractions

La Alhambra, Granada

Dating back to the 12th century, it is an impressive palace and fortress complex in the south.

La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona

The famous large and unfinished catholic basilica designed by Gaudí in 1882.

Puerta del Sol, Madrid

This square is one of the best known and busiest places in the city.

Plaza de España, Sevilla

Landmark example of the combination of styles in the Spanish architecture.

Castillo de la Peralada, Gerona

This castle was built in the 14th century and it's one of Cataluña's architectural, historical and cultural jewels.
